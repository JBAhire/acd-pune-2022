Sponsorship Proposal

Dear sir,


Greetings from AWS UG Pune!



I, on behalf of, AWS User Group (AWS UG Pune) writing to you to seek if your company would be interested in being an integral part of the AWS Community Day Pune 2020 as an honourable sponsor.


AWS Community Day events are community organized full day events and supported by AWS. Talks feature technical discussions led by expert AWS users and community leaders from around the world. Talks covering several interesting topics presented with real-world cases. Topics also cover a wide variety of interests and technical levels, so everyone should have interesting day from the scheduled offering. It also offers a great opportunity for networking between people and allow to connect the academic and industry environments.


Started in 2019, AWS UG Pune is around 4300 member strong community that provides the platform to meet local developers with similar interests in technology, attend talks on a wide range of technical topics, and learn new skills through hands-on workshops.


AWS Community Day Pune 2020 will be held on Saturday, 8th February 2020, at the Hyatt Regency, Pune. 


In order to organize this kind of event, we are offering partnership opportunities to IT companies interested in supporting us. For this reason, we prepared a range of sponsorship packages for potential partners with increasing contributions and visibility benefits. (For more details, please find sponsorship proposal attached below.) We believe AWS Community Day Pune will be a great opportunity for recruiters and for getting in touch with the local IT experts.


AWS Community Day Pune is organized entirely on a voluntary basis and does not involve any monetary benefits to the community. The event will be with minimal ticket charges and open to everyone. The aim is to provide the exchange of knowledge and bring together the developers community of Pune area.


Please note, Some things have some technical deadlines so we invite you, if interested, to get in touch with us as soon as possible to avoid complications.


Looking forward to seeking your collaboration for the AWS Community Day Pune 2020.
